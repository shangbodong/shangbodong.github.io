Openings

We are always on the lookout for highly motivated research assistant professor, PostDocs, Ph.D. students, research assistant, and research interns interested in working on cutting-edge problems in wireless communications, wireless networks, and machine learning. Interested applicants are encouraged to contact Prof. Shang with a resume and corresponding materials.

Postdoctoral Recruitment

1. Recruitment Plan
(1) Recruitment direction: 6G, wireless communications, wireless networks.

2. Recruitment Requirements
(1) The age is generally not more than 35 years old, and the applicant has obtained or is about to obtain a doctoral degree in the past 3 years;
(2) Have a good academic background, strong independent research and scientific research ability, and be preferred to publish high-level papers as the first author or corresponding author or preside over national, provincial and ministerial scientific research projects;
(3) Have good English reading, communication and writing skills, good teamwork and communication and coordination skills.

3. Postdoc Programs
Postdocs are awarded a Postdoc certificate from Tsinghua University or University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) after completing the Postdoc program.

         

4. Salary
(1) Comprehensive annual salary before tax 400,000-600,000 yuan/year (including local government postdoctoral living allowance);
(2) During the station, you can apply to the local government for scientific research funding of up to 200,000 yuan;
(3) For those who have received funding from China Postdoctoral Science Foundation and provincial postdoctoral scientific research projects, Ningbo city will provide 1:1 supporting funds for various projects;
(4) Postdoctoral fellows who leave to work in Ningbo can enjoy subsidies of up to 600,000 yuan;

5. Welfare
(1) Pay insurances and housing fund (endowment insurance, medical insurance, unemployment insurance, work-related injury insurance and maternity insurance, and housing provident fund);
(2) Can apply to stay in Zhenhai District Talent Apartment (doctoral students enjoy rent discounts);
(3) Enjoy other equal welfare benefits for faculty and staff of the Eastern Institute of Technology (EIT), Ningbo.

6. Materials Required for Postdoctoral Application
Postdoctoral applicants should send the following electronic version of the application materials to Prof. Shang's email. The subject of the email should indicate "Apply for Postdoc + Name" or "博士后申请+姓名".
(1) Chinese or English resume (including research interests, research achievements, honors and awards, etc.);
(2) Three representative papers;
(3) Other materials that can prove the applicant's scientific research ability and entry conditions.

Ph.D. Students Recruitment

1. Recruitment plan
(1) Recruitment direction: 6G, wireless communications, wireless networks, machine learning, Internet of things.

2. PhD Programs
(1) Doctoral students are jointly trained by Eastern Institute of Technology (EIT), Ningbo and Shanghai Jiao Tong University (1+3) or The Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU) (2+2) or University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) (1+3), with a dual supervision system, and will be awarded doctoral degrees from Shanghai Jiao Tong University or The Hong Kong Polytechnic University after graduation or University of Science and Technology of China.

       

(2) Three selection methods are adopted: "undergraduate direct doctorate", "master's and doctor's continuous study" and "application-assessment system". The basic study period of direct doctoral students is 5 years, and the others are 4 years, and the admission category is non-directional employment, and the study form is full-time.

(3) To stimulate the scientific research and innovation of doctoral students, we offer a stipend of 100,000-200,000 yuan/year, a competitive scholarship of 30,000 yuan/year, and group incentives with no cap for joint doctoral students during their studies.

3. Training Modes
(1) EIT-SJTU Joint Ph.D. Program: The Ph.D. students under the EIT-SJTU joint Ph.D. Program to register their status at Shanghai Jiao Tong University. In the first year, they study the courses at Shanghai Jiao Tong University. Since the second year, they are mainly engaged in scientific research related to the academic dissertations in Eastern Institute of Technology (EIT), Ningbo. If the students under joint Ph.D. Program meet the graduation conditions of Shanghai Jiao Tong University, they will be awarded the graduation certificate of SJTU. If the students meet the requirements of Shanghai Jiao Tong University on doctoral degree, they will be granted with the doctoral degree of SJTU.

(2) EIT-PolyU Joint Ph.D. Program: During the two-year study in Hong Kong, the Ph.D. students under joint PhD Program will be entitled of the same amount of stipend as the Ph.D. students of Hong Kong Polytechnic University. During your study at EIT, we will provide you with stipend and competitive scholarship according to relevant national regulations so that you can concentrate on your study and research. If the students meet the requirements of Hong Kong Polytechnic University on doctoral degree, they will be granted with the doctoral degree of PolyU.

(3) EIT-USTC Joint Ph.D. Program: The Ph.D. students under the EIT-USTC joint Ph.D. Program to register their status at University of Science and Technology of China. In the first year, they study the courses at University of Science and Technology of China. Since the second year, they are mainly engaged in scientific research related to the academic dissertations in Eastern Institute of Technology (EIT), Ningbo. If the students under joint Ph.D. Program meet the graduation conditions of University of Science and Technology of China, they will be awarded the graduation certificate of USTC. If the students meet the requirements of University of Science and Technology of China on doctoral degree, they will be granted with the doctoral degree of USTC.

Research Assistant Recruitment

1. Recruitment plan
(1) Recruitment direction: 6G, wireless communications, wireless networks, machine learning, Internet of things.

2. Job Responsibilities
(1) Participate in or lead laboratory scientific research projects;
(2) Assist postdoctoral and graduate students in scientific research.

3. Application Conditions
(1) Generally require master's degree or above. Outstanding candidates with bachelor's degree are available;
(2) Have certain relevant research experience;
(3) Good English reading and communication skills and strong teamwork skills.

4. Benefits
(1) Monthly salary of 4000-6000 yuan/month (Monthly rent is around 1400 yuan for a studio);
(2) Pay insurances and housing fund (endowment insurance, medical insurance, unemployment insurance, work-related injury insurance and maternity insurance, and housing provident fund);
(3) Excellent research assistants will be recommended to transfer to a Ph.D. student.

5. Contact
(1) Applicants can send their resumes, transcripts, scientific research achievements, etc. to the Prof. Shang's email, and the subject of the email should indicate "Apply for Research Assistant + name" or "科研助理申请+姓名".
